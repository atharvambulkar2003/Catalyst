About Us

The mission of Catalyst, the writers' club, is to cultivate a vibrant community where students can freely express their creativity through writing. The club aims to provide a supportive platform for students to develop their literary skills, engage in meaningful discussions, and collaborate on various writing projects. Through workshops, events, and peer feedback, Catalyst encourages members to refine their craft, explore diverse forms of writing, and share their unique voices with a broader audience.

What is the purpose of the club

The purpose of Catalyst, the writers' club, is to promote the art of writing among students by providing opportunities for creative expression and skill development. The club serves as a platform for aspiring writers to hone their craft, share their work with peers, and receive constructive feedback. It also aims to foster a culture of literary appreciation and encourage collaboration through group projects, writing contests, and community events, ultimately nurturing a deeper passion for writing.
